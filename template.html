We are a fund-raising organization with the goal of assisting other non-profit beagle rescue organizations who are engaged in the work of rescuing, rehabilitating and rehoming beagle dogs.

We provide re-imbursement for some of the expenses involved in this work. The rescued dog must be a beagle. Funds are available only after the dog has been placed into a permanent home.  Receiving funds from ABRN is a 2-step process. FIRST fill out the form "ABRN Account Application". Once you have received notification that your account has been established, use the "Request for Reimbursement" form to request funds.
