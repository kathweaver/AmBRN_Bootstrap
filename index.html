We are a fund-raising organization with the goal of assisting other non-profit beagle rescue organizations who are engaged in the work of rescuing, rehabilitating and rehoming beagle dogs.

We provide reimbursement for some of the expenses involved in this work. The rescued dog must be a beagle. Funds are available only after the dog has been placed into a permanent home.  Receiving funds from ABRN is a 2-step process. FIRST fill out the form "ABRN Account Application". Once you have received notification that your account has been established, use the "Request for Reimbursement" form to request funds.

AmBRN is a tax exempt publicly supported organization as described under Section 501(c)(3) and 509(a) of the Internal Revenue Code
